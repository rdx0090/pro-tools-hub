At Pro Tools Hub, your privacy is our highest priority. This policy outlines how we handle information.

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or files from our users. All tools on this website (including the image compressor, QR generator, etc.) operate entirely on the client-side, meaning all processing is done within your own web browser on your own device. Your files are never uploaded to our or any third-party server.

2. Third-Party Libraries (CDNs)

Our website uses common, trusted third-party libraries (like SheetJS, jsPDF, etc.) hosted on Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) to provide functionality. These CDNs may collect standard, non-personally identifiable information like your IP address for security and analytics, as is standard practice for most websites. We do not have access to this data.

3. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. We may use local storage in your browser only to save your preferences (like settings for a tool), but this information is stored on your device and is not accessible to us.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
