Privacy Policy for Magic Kit

Last updated: [9 JUNE 2025]

Welcome to Magic Kit. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information that is collected and recorded by Magic Kit and how we use it.

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from our users. All the tools available on Magic Kit (such as the Image Compressor, Background Remover, etc.) perform their operations entirely within your web browser on your own device ("client-side processing").

The files you upload are **never** sent to our servers or any third-party servers, with the exception of tools that explicitly state they use a third-party API.

2. Third-Party APIs

Some of our tools, like the "Background Remover", use third-party APIs (e.g., Clipdrop) to function. When you use these specific tools:

  • Your file is securely sent to the third-party service for processing.
  • We do not log your API key or the content of your files.
  • We encourage you to review the privacy policy of the respective third-party service (e.g., Clipdrop) to understand how they handle your data.

3. Log Files & Analytics

Magic Kit follows a standard procedure of using log files provided by our hosting provider (Vercel). These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, date and time stamp, and referring/exit pages. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. The purpose of the information is for analyzing trends, administering the site, and gathering demographic information for our own understanding.

4. Children's Information

Our website does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13.

5. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
