Privacy Policy for MagicKit.space

Last Updated: June 9, 2024

Welcome to MagicKit! Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our website and tools.

Client-Side Processing: Our "No-Upload" Promise

The vast majority of our tools (including the Image Compressor, Image Enhancer, QR Code Generator, File Compressor, etc.) operate entirely on your device. This is called **client-side processing**.

This means: Your files are NEVER uploaded to our servers. All the magic happens directly in your web browser. We have no access to your files, and they do not leave your computer. This is the core of our commitment to your privacy.

Third-Party APIs (For AI Tools)

A few of our advanced tools, such as the **AI Background Remover**, rely on specialized third-party APIs (like remove.bg or Clipdrop) to function. For these specific tools:

  • Your image file is securely sent to the respective third-party service for processing.
  • The file is used solely to perform the requested action (e.g., remove the background) and is then returned to you.
  • We do not store your original or processed files. However, these third-party services operate under their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them if you have concerns.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or location.

Analytics

We may use privacy-friendly analytics services to understand how our website is used (e.g., which tools are most popular). This data is anonymized and aggregated, and it cannot be used to identify you personally.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@magickit.space (Yeh email abhi bana nahi hai, aapko banana hoga).
